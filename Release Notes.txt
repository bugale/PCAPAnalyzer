PCAPAnalyzer Release Notes
--------------------------
BETA 7:
	Features Added:
	 -The number of PIDs with\without PCR which were processed at least twice is now written in the Statistics file.
	 -The number of PIDs with\without PCR which were processed at least twice in two distinct packets is now written in the Statistics file.
	 -The PIDs with\without PCR which were processed at least twice are now shown in the PID-Bitrate\PCR-Offset file.
	 -The PIDs with\without PCR which were processed at least twice in two distinct packets are now shown in the PID-Bitrate\PCR-Offset file.
